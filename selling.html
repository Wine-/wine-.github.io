How Can I Sell My Wine?

Please contact us at email@wine-raiders.com or at 555-555-5555 with the following information available:

1) The Vintage, wine name, and producer; any vineyard designations that apply; such as whether it is a reserve.

2) The number of bottles, size (750ml, magnum, double magnum, etc.), condition of the bottle(s)- fill levels (ullage,) label condition, signs of seepage, in short any condition that will affect the value of the bottle. Any agreed upon price for your collection is subject to review once we have the wines in house and can make our own visual inspection. Please also provide photos of the wine under sufficient lighting.

3) Once we agree on a price for your collection,you will recieve a written contract via email as well as instruction on the shipment of your wine to us. Note: (All shipping materials are provided by wine-raiders and are fully insured. We cover all shipping/logistics costs). For large aquisitions be prepared for us to come to you.

4) As soon as wine is recieved and inspected we will overnight you a certified bank check or pay you under another, agreed-upon method. Payment is immediate, and satisfaction is guaranteed.
